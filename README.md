## 🛠 Features

*   **Market Review:** Automates the gathering of daily market data to save you time.
*   **Risk Management:** Sets clear rules for position sizing and trade exposure.
*   **Trading Plans:** Provides templates to define your strategy before you enter the market.
*   **Trade Journal:** Records every trade so you can review your performance later.
*   **Continuous Improvement:** Tracks your progress to help you refine your skills over time.

## 🚀 How to Use the Application

Once you finish the setup, open the application by double-clicking the icon on your desktop. The first window you see requests your API keys if you connect to market data services.

### Setting Up Your Workflow
The software uses a dashboard layout. You will see tabs at the top of the interface:

*   **Review Tab:** Use this to pull today's market highlights. The software fetches data from the Vietnam stock market sources.
*   **Planner Tab:** Create a new plan here. Enter your entry price, stop-loss level, and profit target. The tool saves these details for your records.
*   **Journal Tab:** Every time you exit a trade, return to this tab. Input the final price and result. The tool calculates your win rate and total profit or loss automatically.
*   **Risk Dashboard:** This area shows your current exposure. If a trade plan exceeds your risk limit, the tool alerts you.

### Adding Data
You can import past trade data from Excel files. Click "File," select "Import," and choose your document. The software maps the columns automatically to match your journal.
